# **App Name**: BarMate

## Core Features:

- Product Management: Simple product creation, allowing staff to easily add and edit items in the bar's inventory, along with their prices.
- Point of Sale (POS): Clear interface to record orders. Calculate totals based on selected items.
- Payment Processing: Select payment method (cash, card, or Pix). The system provides the correct change to be given back when the customer pays in cash.
- Sales Reporting: Log of daily transactions, which can be filtered. It shows gross income per day and other metrics to provide valuable data on the bar's sales performance.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) to convey trust and stability, crucial for handling financial transactions.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) for a clean and professional look, ensuring readability and focus on the content.
- Accent color: Amber (#FFC107) to highlight important actions and information, such as payment methods and totals.
- Use a clear, sans-serif font to maintain readability on the product list and totals display.
- Crisp, simple icons for product categories and payment options for ease of use.
- Simple, intuitive layout for ease of use in a busy environment. Display totals prominently and separate sections with whitespace.
